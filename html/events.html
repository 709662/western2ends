Markt Kabaal - Tinderdome

Date : 16 feb 2024

Location : Westergas

Immerse yourself in a unique experience at the Tinderdome Festival, where love is more than just swiping! This one-day celebration is designed to challenge and celebrate your view of love, with a twist of fun, music, and self-discovery. Music & Dance: Live music from Kiss My Knoblauch and dancing, especially lots of dancing and flirting. DJs are yet to be announced. Food: Enjoy delicious food from local delicacies. Because love goes through the stomach, right? Workshops: Dive into engaging workshops that will help you explore and broaden your ideas about love. From relationship strategies to self-discovery, we've got it all!
